As a participant in NACDEC-VI, I contributed to the conceptualization and design of an autonomous aircraft intended for delivering patients or essential medical supplies during critical emergency situations[cite: 1].

### Systems Engineering & MATLAB Modeling
The preliminary and conceptual design phases required a comprehensive systems engineering approach. Using **MATLAB** programming, I conducted detailed evaluations across multiple aerospace disciplines[cite: 1]:
*   **Weight Estimation & Payload:** Evaluated UAV payload capacities to ensure mission feasibility[cite: 1].
*   **Aerodynamic Analysis:** Analyzed various aircraft configurations for maximum aerodynamic efficiency in both aerial and marine contexts[cite: 1].
*   **Propulsion, Stability, & Avionics:** Performed rigorous propulsion sizing, stability and control analysis, and avionics system evaluations[cite: 1].
