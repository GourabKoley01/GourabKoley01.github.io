### Noise Reduction through Iterative Design
Beyond propulsion efficiency, I am actively researching the aeroacoustic properties of fluid flow around subsonic nozzles[cite: 1]. The primary objective of this project is to conduct a comparative study on iterative pattern designs to achieve significant noise reduction[cite: 1].

### Advanced Meshing and OpenFOAM
This project heavily utilizes the **OpenFOAM** framework for computational fluid dynamics analysis[cite: 1]. A critical component of simulating aeroacoustics accurately is the quality of the computational grid. I generate highly structured grids using a combination of **snappyHexMesh** and **Pointwise** to capture the intricate acoustic pressure waves and turbulent fluctuations near the nozzle exit[cite: 1].
